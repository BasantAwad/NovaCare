1, Case of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a computer system that mimics human intellectual abilities.
When new knowledge or information is given, it improves existing knowledge with
algorithms such as learning, pattern recognition, and feature analysis. In the past,
AI required high-performance computers, So, to use artificial intelligence in
terminal devices such as smartphones and vehicles, we had to create an AI server,
However, with the development of small high-performance hardware and high-
efficiency algorithms, artificial intelligence can be implemented even in small
terminal devices, In addition, On-Device Al, which has built-in artificial
intelligence without the need to access an AI server, has appeared, This will be
explained in more details later.

Early AI was lacking in computer performance compared to various theories. So it
was just a level of entering large amounts of data and making small decisions.
However, as computer hardware technology advances and many artificial
intelligence theories have undergone many improvements in the modern era, and
they are growing rapidly.

3. AIoT SerBot Prime X Control

AloT SerBot PrimeX uses a special wheel called Omni Wheel. Omniwheel is a form
of one large wheel by joining several small wheels together. The direction of
rotation of the small wheel is horizontal, and when connecting with a large wheel,
the direction can be placed vertically so that it can move back and forth, left and
right. With these properties, it can change the direction of movement without
rotating, and also can rotate in place. In other words, it is free to turn compared to
a vehicle.

There are two main types of movement using omni wheels, It is to move by changing
direction without rotating, and to change direction by rotating like a vehicle. For
convenience, we will refer to each as a fixed axis movement and steering movement.
In this course, students practice fixed axis movement and steering movement using
a library made in advance by Hanback Electronics. You can refer to the appendix

page for a theoretical explanation of both transfer methods.

Figure 30 Fixed Axis Movement(Left) and Steering Movement(Right)

AloT SerBot PrimeX can be easily controlled using the SerBot class of the Pop.Pilot
library, In this library, there are methods to control movement and methods to read
6-axis sensor values,

3,2, LIDAR Sensor

LIDAR igh vetection and Renging 1S a device that detects objects from a long distance, LiDAR,
rotating 360 degrees, can measure angle and distance values, In other words, it can
detect the presence of an object in a certain direction and a certain distance, LIDAR
mounted on AloT SerBot PrimeX measures up to 12m every 0.45° and scans 10
revolutions per second,

3,2,1, LIDAR Basic Control

LIDAR can be used with the Pop.LiDAR library. However, the LiDAR sensor
transmits real-time data only once in one-to-one communication. Therefore, if
multiple processes receive LIDAR values at the same time, there is a high
probability that data reception will fail. The main methods of controlling equipment
are:

¢ —connect() : LIDAR connection

* startMotor(): Starts LIDAR scanning motor

* —stopMotor() ; Stops LIDAR scanning motor

* getVertors : Returns a list of vector value tuples (Degree, Distance, Quality) scanned during one
rotation in ascending order by angle

* —getXY() : Returns a list of coordinate value tuples (X, Y) scanned during one rotation in ascending order
by angle

* getMap(size, limit_distance) : Returns a list of coordinate value tuples (X, Y) scanned during one
rotation in ascending order by angle
- size: The size of the map to return. Default: (300, 300)

- _ limit_distance : The maximum distance to be expressed on the map. If set to 2000, only values less

than 2000mm are reflected in the map. Default: 12000

5.3. Text-to-Speech

The TTS texto speecy Which is used to convert text to speech, is a technique which
makes artificial voice, It records the speech of a person selected as a voice model,
divides it into a regular unit of speech, saves it with a code to the synthesizer, and
only the needed specific speech units are recombined to create artificial speech. In
general, it is difficult to synthesize the segmental sound of a voice, so the artificial
voice is synthesized by recording the front and back of the voice together based
on the boundary of the segmented sound.

Text-to-speech systems are divided into front-end and back-end. The front-end
normalizes the text entered by the user to determine how to handle numbers or
omitted expressions. Then, each word is converted to a phonetic symbol, and the
result of division into words, idioms, and sentences is sent to the back-end. On the
basis of this, the rhyme and the like are adjusted to make the voice more natural,
and then synthesized and output as real voice data through back-end.

5.3.1. Google’s Text to Speech Converter

Google’s Text-to-Speech Converter started as a multilingual screen reader
application developed to read text on Android smartphones. Currently, it is ported
to WaveNet of DeepMind, an AI subsidiary, and is evolving into a cloud version.

DeepMind's AI speech synthesis technology is particularly sophisticated and
realistic. Most speech synthesizers, including Apple's Siri, use link synthesis.
However, WaveNet stores individual syllables, combines them to form words and
sentences, and generates speech by machine learning. Waveforms are generated
based on human voice database for playback at a rate of 24,000 samples per second,
and the final result includes even subtle processing such as accents,

7,3, Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning relies on chance at the beginning of learning, and gradually
learns to maximize reward. Therefore, the initial learning error is very high, and
the error decreases rapidly over time. No learning data processing is required, and
only the necessary choice is learned, so it possible for it to learn a lot in a short
time in a high performance environment, However, because the initial learning
process happens by accident, the final learning model may differ depending on the
initial learning model, Learning characteristics are very similar to unsupervised
learning, but they are different,

It is suitable for data simulation purposes rather than data identification because it
learns for what purpose and which option is the most reasonable. For example,
unsupervised learning learns to distinguish the types of fruits, while reinforcement

learning learns which fruits have high sugar content. Examples of reinforcement
learning include Google's AlphaGo and Boston Dynamics’ walking robots.

8, On-Device AI

Artificial intelligence is not only massive, but requires high-performance
computing power. Previously, edge devices collected data and sentit to an Al server
for processing. However, this method is very expensive to configure the basic
environment in addition to security problems and network delay problems. In
addition, there was a problem such as taking up a large space for equipment
installation.

As technology advances, the level of performance required to implement artificial
intelligence has decreased, and the performance of computing devices has
improved, resulting in lower prices. As a result, we have solved security and
network delay problems by embedding artificial intelligence in edge devices, and
made the environment configuration cost cheaper. In addition, the device became
as small as a palm. The artificial intelligence built into the edge device is called
On-device Al.

With on-device AI technology, artificial intelligence can be built into many devices
even in small spaces. This soon became an opportunity to incorporate artificial
intelligence into the Internet of Things environment. And it made it possible to
realize AloT which is combining artificial intelligence and the Internet of Things.

Representative examples of on-device Al are home appliances such as TVs,
refrigerators, and air conditioners.

TV with on-device AI analyzes user's viewing pattern and recommends channel or
movie, In addition, it can perform functions such as turning off the power during
periods of low viewing frequency and switching to sleep mode during periods of
high viewing frequency for preparing quick response. ;

In case of refrigerator, there is a function to save electricity by increasing the
cooling performance during times of high use and lowering the cooling

stored food for each internal compartment and setting it to the optimum
temperature.

When applied to an air conditioner, it recognizes a person and sends the wind only

temperature preferred by the individual.

Furthermore, the technology that artificial intelligence controls the vehicle using
data such as cameras and ultrasonic waves is called Autonomous Driving. Among

C. MFCC

The key to preprocessing voice data is to distinguish valid data from noise. Noise
generally has a constant level and pattern in frequency and amplitude, and voice
consists of various waveforms and irregular patterns. Therefore, if it is
preprocessed to reveal the characteristics of the voice, it will be the best data that
minimizes noise and can easily analyze the voice.
